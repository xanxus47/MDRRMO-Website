DISASTER PREPAREDNESS TIPS

Before a Disaster

📌 Family Preparedness

  • ✅ Hold family meetings to discuss emergency plans.
  • ✅ Identify safe spots in your home for different hazards (earthquakes, typhoons).
  • ✅ Teach children how to call for help (911, MDRRMO hotline).

📌 Emergency Kit Essentials

  • 💧 Water: 1 gallon per person per day (3-day supply).
  • 🍞 Food: Non-perishable items (canned goods, energy bars).
  • ⚡ Power: Flashlights, batteries, portable charger.
  • 🏥 First Aid: Bandages, medicines, sanitizer.
  • 📄 Documents: IDs, insurance, emergency contacts in a waterproof bag.

During a Disaster

🌪️ Typhoon / Flood

  • ⚠️ Stay indoors, away from windows.
  • ⚠️ Avoid walking/driving through floodwaters.
  • ⚠️ Turn off electricity if flooding occurs.

🌍 Earthquake

  • 🔄 Drop, Cover, Hold: Protect yourself under sturdy furniture.
  • 🚫 Avoid doorways (they’re not safer than other parts).
  • 🏃 Evacuate if near the coast (tsunami risk).

After a Disaster

🔍 Safety Checks

  • 🔌 Check for gas leaks, electrical damage.
  • 🚧 Avoid unstable buildings or bridges.
  • 📢 Listen to official updates before returning home.

💙 Community Support

  • 🤝 Help neighbors (especially elderly/disabled).
  • 📞 Report missing persons to authorities.
  • 🗑️ Separate waste for proper disposal.

Advanced Preparedness

📱 Tech & Alerts

  • 📲 Download disaster alert apps (e.g., NOAH, Red Cross).
  • 🔔 Enable emergency alerts on your phone.
  • 🛰️ Keep a battery-powered radio for news.

🚗 Evacuation Plan

  • 🗺️ Know multiple evacuation routes.
  • ⛽ Keep your car’s gas tank half-full.
  • 🧳 Pack a "go bag" with essentials.
