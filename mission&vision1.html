MISSION & VISION

Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (MDRRMO)

Magsaysay, Occidental Mindoro

OUR VISION

"Always be prepared and attain zero casualty."

We envision a municipality where every individual, family, and institution is empowered to face disasters with confidence. Through proactive measures, community engagement, and effective leadership, we strive for a future where hazards do not result in loss of lives, destruction of property, or harm to the environment.

OUR MISSION

"To avoid hazards and mitigate their potential impact by reducing vulnerabilities and exposure while enhancing the capabilities of communities."

We commit to effectively and efficiently managing disasters to minimize loss of lives, damage to property, and environmental harm through the harmonized efforts of all stakeholders at the local level.

Risk Reduction

Identifying hazards and minimizing vulnerabilities through comprehensive assessment and planning.

Preparedness

Strengthening community resilience through education, training, and early warning systems.

Response Efficiency

Ensuring swift, organized, and well-coordinated disaster response operations.

Recovery & Rehabilitation

Supporting affected communities towards sustainable recovery and rebuilding.

Multi-Stakeholder Collaboration

Uniting local government, NGOs, private sectors, and citizens in disaster risk management.
