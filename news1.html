Detailed Description

MDRRMO, Magsaysay, Occidental Mindoro installed a warning signs tarpaulin to 12 barangays of Magsaysay, Occidental Mindoro in order to read or gave awareness with the use of the said materials. It will helps all the peole of every barangay of what their preparedness needs in order to survive to natural disaster in case of emergency.

With this newly installed warning signs we the people of Mindoro survive together. It will reduce emergencies and casualties to each individual of every barangay. This also helps the fiherman and farmers to be aware for every season.

Also the riding vehicles benefits with this newly installion because it gives warning in order to minimize their speed in all accident prone area. With this implementation it helps minimize vehicles incident.
